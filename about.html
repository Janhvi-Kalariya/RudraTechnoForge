About Us

Rudra Techno Forge is a leader in delivering innovative industrial and auto-component solutions, specializing in closed die steel forging, upset forging, and ring rolling forging.

“The company offers forging services in carbon steel, alloy steel, and stainless steel, with products supplied in forged, heat-treated, pre-machined, or fully machined conditions.”

Rudra Techno Forge has a production capacity of 650 metric tons per month, with ring rolling capabilities up to 360 mm outer diameter and drop forging capabilities up to 20 kg per piece.

The company specializes in complex machined forgings from carbon and stainless steel, serving various engineering segments competitively.
